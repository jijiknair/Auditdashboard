Infection Prevention and Control Program

Ref No Elements Activities Scoring
D SI O
1.1 At least one infection control focal point trained in IPC is available to manage the facility infection control program
1.2 The Infection control focal point is up to date with the current national guidelines and implemented in the facility
1.3 Updated GCC infection prevention and control manual is available and accessible for all HCWs
1.4 Facility has system for early detection and management of potentially infectious persons at initial points of patient encounter (e.g. respiratory infectious patients)
1.5 IPC annual action plan is written according to risk assessment and implemented
1.6 There is documented IPC meeting with the facility incharge/ head to discuss IPC issues and risk assessment at least bio-annually
1.7 Report to IPC section in DGHS for any; communicable diseases, infectious exposure, healthcare-associated infection (HAI), inoculation injury and audit result
